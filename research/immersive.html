Immersive Micromanipulation System

In this study, we have proposed a immersive micromanipulation system that improves operability through immersive micro-space for the purpose of simplifying the operation of microinjection.




Reference

  • K. Yokoe, T. Aoyama, T. Fujishiro, M. Takeuchi and Y. Hasegawa, "An immersive micro‑manipulation system using real‑time 3D imaging microscope and 3D operation interface for high‑speed and accurate micro‑manipulation", ROBOMECH Journal, 9, 16, 2022.
  • K. Yokoe, T. Aoyama, T. Fujishiro, M. Takeuchi and Y.Hasegawa, "High-speed and High-accuracy Immersive Micro Manipulation System Using 3D Imaging Microscope and Glove-type Operation Interfaces", IEEE International Conference on Advanced Robotics and Its Social Impacts, pp.107-108, 2021.
