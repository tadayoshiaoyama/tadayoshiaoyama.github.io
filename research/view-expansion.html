Micromanipulation System with View-expansion Microscope

In this research, we are developing a system that enables easy micromanipulation by simultaneously presenting Large FOV images and high-resolution images. We aim to develop a system that allows even beginners to easily perform micromanipulation.




Reference

  • T. Aoyama, S. Takeno, K. Yokoe, K. Hano, M. Takasu, M. Takeuchi and Y. Hasegawa, "Micromanipulation System Capable of Simultaneously Presenting High-Resolution and Large Field-of-View Images in Real-Time", IEEE Access, vol.11, pp.34274-34285, 2023.
  • T. Aoyama, S. Takeno, K. Hano, M. Takasu, M. Takeuchi, and Y. Hasegawa, "View-expansion Microscope System with Real-time High-resolution Imaging for Simplified Microinjection Experiments", IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation, pp.12961-12966, 2021.
